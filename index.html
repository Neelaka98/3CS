LOLC Finance PLC

www.lolcfinance.com

LOLC Finance is the largest Non-Bank Financial Institution in Sri Lanka, specialising in a wide array of financial services such as Savings and Fixed Deposits, Leasing, Islamic Banking, Micro Leasing, Loans and Cards. LOLC Finance has many years of experience in the financial sector.The expertise they have amassed in the industry helps them to provide their customers with an unparalleled set of services.

With a focus on user-friendliness, the new LOLC Finance site is simple and easy to navigate. It is mobile responsive and compatible with all devices, browsers and platforms. The design 3CS presented for LOLC Finance reflects our evolving design language and philosophy. The site offers many interactive features including a branch locator, rate calculators and access to online portals and the mobile app. The previous design of the LOLC website was also done by 3CS several years ago.

  • Date: May 2018
  • Client: LOLC Finance
  • Category: Web Design
